2023 - 2024 school year 's theme: Learn smart - Go global - Be a happy you

21 August, 2023

Maintaining all the activities following the theme "Learn smart - Go global - Be a happy you" in the school year 2023 - 2024, Olympia strives for Olympians' invaluable experience, such as a variety of global activities, many international playgrounds for English, Science, Math,... and to give Olympians room to research and be creative at this happy school.

2023 - 2024 is the year Olympia celebrates its 20th anniversary, marking a long journey in developing bilingual programs that meet international accreditation standards. Two decades of pursuing the philosophy of respecting differences, Olympia teachers have been helping generations of students get ready for life and apply successfully for top domestic and international universities.
